About Us

ChangeMakers Mindcare was founded by Samuel Oyewole Ajayi with a simple but powerful vision: to make mental health care affordable, accessible, and stigma-free for young people.

Backed by a passionate team of clinical psychologists, psychiatrists, and counselors, we provide anonymous, professional support that helps students and youth overcome stress, anxiety, and everyday mental health challenges.

✨Together, we are changing the story of mental health — one student at a time✨
